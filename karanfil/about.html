About Me

Hello, I am Furkan Karanfil, a second-year student in Visual Communication Design at TED University. On this website, you can view the designs I have completed so far.

I started my design journey with university and have been continuously developing myself in the field of visual communication design since then. I work on various projects, from poster design to stop-motion videos, aiming to provide effective and innovative solutions in each project.

Throughout my university education, I strive to apply the knowledge and experiences I have gained to my personal projects and academic work, continuously aiming for growth. I emphasize the importance of enriching designs by combining research, creativity, and technical skills during the design process.
